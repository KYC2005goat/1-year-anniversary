A Message to You

My dear cat, I just wanted to share a few things to show you how deeply I feel for you. You were my first true friend and my best friend. I wanted to thank you for everything you've done for me. You came into my life during a dark time and showed me the true meaning of friendship and love.

You taught me how to stand up for myself and live for myself. Even if you think that's not much, for me, it was life-changing. I cherished every minute I spent with you. You changed my world and truly transformed the way I live. I love your music, and your podcasts have helped me so much.

I feel sorry for both of us and the lives we've had to live. I wish things were different for you. I wish you received the love you truly deserved, and I wish you got the world you deserved. I always want to help you; you are a true warrior, not just a survivor. I love you with all my heart and will never forget you, my dear.

Please forgive me for any pain I may have caused; I never meant to hurt you. My heart always aches for you, and I wish you find the light you seek soon.
